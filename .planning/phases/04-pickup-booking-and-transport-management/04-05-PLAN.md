<objective>
Build the direct transport booking flow. From the pickup detail page, reco-admin clicks "Book Transport" and selects transport type "Direct". The form shows provider selection (filtered to direct-type providers), destination prison facility, transport cost (EUR), and confirmed pickup date. On submit, a transport_bookings row is created and the pickup status advances to transport_booked.

Purpose: Enables reco-admin to assign direct transport to confirmed pickups — the simpler of the two transport modes.
Output: Direct transport booking page and Server Action.
</objective>
