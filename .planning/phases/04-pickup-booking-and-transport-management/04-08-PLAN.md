<objective>
Build the transport provider portal. Transport users with platform access see their assigned pickups, active shipments, and completed deliveries. They can update shipment status, add delivery notes, and upload proof of delivery. RLS ensures they only see pickups for their linked clients and cannot access pricing, prison data, or other providers' data.

Purpose: Gives transport providers self-service access to their assigned work without exposing sensitive operational data.
Output: Transport portal pages and Server Actions for status/POD updates.
</objective>
